Safety in Construction

Hazards in Construction

• Risk of falling from heights
• Electrical hazards and shocks
• Injuries from heavy machinery
• Slips, trips, and falls
• Exposure to dust and harmful materials

"M.A. Building Construction ensures all hazards are identified in advance and preventive measures are implemented."

Safety Protocols

• Daily safety meetings (Toolbox Talks)
• Mandatory PPE (Helmet, Shoes, Jackets, Gloves)
• Safety harness for height work
• Trained operators only
• Regular site inspections

“Safety First, Work Always”

Common Accidents

• Machinery-related injuries
• Electric shocks
